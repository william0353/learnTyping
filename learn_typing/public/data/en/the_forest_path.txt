A narrow path winds deeper into the forest, where light and shadow meet.
Leaves whisper softly under every careful step, like secrets being shared.
Tall trees stand close together, forming a green ceiling above the path.
Sunlight breaks through in thin golden lines that move with the wind.
The air is cool and fresh, filled with the smell of earth and wood.
Somewhere nearby, water flows over stones in a steady quiet rhythm.
Small insects move through the grass, invisible until they suddenly appear.
Every turn of the path feels like entering a slightly different world.
Time seems slower here, as if the forest is thinking in silence.
Walking forward feels like reading a story that has no end.
