At night, the city begins to glow like a living map of light.
Windows shine from tall buildings, each one holding a different story.
Cars move through streets, drawing lines of red and white that fade away.
People walk quickly or slowly, each carrying their own invisible world.
The sound of footsteps mixes with distant music and soft traffic noise.
Streetlights stand like quiet watchers along every road and corner.
Reflections shimmer on glass and metal, changing as you move past them.
High above, some windows stay dark, while others stay bright and warm.
Every block feels different, as if the city is made of many moods.
Even in the noise and motion, there is a hidden rhythm that never stops.
